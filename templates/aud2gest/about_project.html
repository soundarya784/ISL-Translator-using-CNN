About The Project


A mutual conversation Interface for Gesture Recognition for aiding Real-Time hand gestures to speech and vice versa by a novel VGG-19 based Model and Optimised Breadth First Search Algorithm. It would provide a platform for the deaf-mute to communicate freely with the rest of the society.
